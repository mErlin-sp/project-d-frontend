Project Background

In today's world, e-commerce plays a key role in economic development, providing convenient and fast access to goods and services for consumers around the world. The growing number of e-commerce platforms such as Amazon, eBay, Alibaba, and others creates a need for effective tools to monitor, collect, and analyze data on products, prices, and market trends. This is especially important for businesses seeking to remain competitive and respond quickly to market changes. In this regard, the development of a web service for collecting information from electronic trading platforms is relevant and necessary.

The reason for this work is the need to automate the processes of collecting and analyzing a large amount of data from various electronic trading platforms. Existing solutions often do not fully meet the needs of users due to their limited functionality, low performance, or lack of integration with other systems. Thus, the development of a web service that enables automatic data collection, processing, and analysis is extremely important for ensuring efficient business operations.

Technologies for collecting and analyzing data from electronic trading platforms are actively developing in the world. The use of web scraping, machine learning, and big data methods allows for high accuracy and speed of information processing. Leading companies and research institutions, such as Google, Amazon Web Services, and IBM, are actively working on creating tools to automate the processes of monitoring and analyzing market data. Scientists and experts in the field of computer science and artificial intelligence are exploring new methods of data collection and processing that can significantly improve the efficiency of working with large amounts of information.

Today, there are a large number of different tools and services for collecting information from electronic trading platforms. However, many of them have limited functionality or do not provide the required level of integration with other systems. Leading scientific institutions and organizations, such as MIT Media Lab and Stanford Artificial Intelligence Laboratory, are actively working on research in the field of data collection and analysis automation, developing new algorithms and technologies that can significantly improve the accuracy and speed of information processing. The web service for collecting information from electronic trading platforms can be used in various areas, including:

  • Business analytics;
  • Marketing;
  • Logistics and inventory management;
  • Financial analysis.
